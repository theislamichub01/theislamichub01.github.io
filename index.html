☪ Help & Info

Is Islamic Hub free to use?
Yes. Core features are free. Some games and extras use an in-app coin system you earn through quizzes and activity.
How do I earn and use coins?
Coins are earned by completing quizzes and daily activities, and can be spent unlocking games in the Halal Games section.
Where do the Quran translations come from?
From established, authentic sources via our Quran Explorer, with reliable backup sources used if one is temporarily unavailable.
Can I bookmark stories or verses?
Yes, use the star icon to bookmark your favorite Quran ayahs and stories for quick access later.
Is the community chat moderated?
Yes, posts follow community guidelines to keep the space respectful and beneficial for everyone.

Islamic Hub is a free educational platform built to make authentic Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone. From Quran exploration and prayer tools to kids' stories and a Muslim community space, our goal is to combine reliable Islamic resources with a modern, easy-to-use experience.

We're community-driven and always improving — built by Muslims, for Muslims, with knowledge, spirituality, and community at the heart of everything we make.
