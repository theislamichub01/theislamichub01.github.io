About These Hadiths

Sahih al-Bukhari: Compiled by Imam Muhammad al-Bukhari (810-870 CE). Contains approximately 7,563 Hadiths (with repetitions), selected from over 600,000 Hadiths through the most rigorous authentication process in Islamic history.

Why These Two Only? These are the only two Hadith collections that ALL Islamic scholars across ALL schools of thought accept as 100% authentic (Sahih). While other collections exist, they may contain weak (Da'if) Hadiths, so we limit ourselves to these two for maximum authenticity.
