üìú Community Guidelines

  • Respect & Kindness: Treat all members with respect and kindness. Disagree with ideas, not people. Remember, we're all brothers and sisters in Islam.
  • Authentic Knowledge: When sharing Islamic knowledge, always provide sources from Quran, Hadith, or verified scholars. Avoid sharing unverified information.
  • No Sectarian Disputes: Focus on what unites us as Muslims. Avoid heated debates about sectarian differences. We're here to learn, not to argue.
  • Protect Privacy: Never share personal information about yourself or others. Keep discussions focused on knowledge and support.
  • Seek Scholars: For serious religious questions, consult qualified scholars. Community discussions are for learning, not issuing fatwas.
  • Report Issues: If you see inappropriate content or behavior, report it to moderators immediately. Help us keep this space pure and beneficial.
  • Good Character: Represent Islam beautifully through your words and actions. Your behavior here is your dawah to others.
